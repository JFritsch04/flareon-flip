How to Play:

Flareon Flip is a small game that revolves around simple math and flipping tiles to get a high score. Along the right side and bottom of the board, you will see two numbers per row/column. The top number designates the sum of the numbers in the row/column and the bottom number designates the amount of flareons(bombs) in the row/column. The goal is to flip all the numbers in that row/column without flipping over a flareon. If a flareon is flipped, you lose. If a number is flipped, your high score will increase by that amount.
